FractConductor was a project we created with Jussi Härkönen, Veli Laamanen and Markus Lepistö at SibHack SibHack organized at Ateneum in Helsinki. The project is a virtual conducting experience combined with amazing fractal graphics. I was in charge of making the Interactive Conductors Baton using a Blobo ball. The player can control both the graphics and the music by waving the baton.
