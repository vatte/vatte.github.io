Immediate Invisible is a wearable electronics fashion collection that I created with fashion designer Liisa Pesonen and composer Samuli Tanner. It was the topic of Liisa's master's thesis. The aim is to generate an evolving soundscape that responds to the involuntarily processes inside the wearer.

All the clothes in the collection are functional. Each of them belong to one of three categories: computer, speaker or physiological sensor. The collection is fully modular: any combination of three clothes of each category can form a functional outfit.
