Teaching

Physical Interaction Design

I've been responsible for teaching the Physical Interaction Design (25425) course at Aalto University's Media Lab Helsinki, where it is a part of the MA studies. On this course, students from different backgrounds are familiarised with technologies and methods of physical computing through hands-on exercises and also by working on their own project, which is presented at the end of the course.
